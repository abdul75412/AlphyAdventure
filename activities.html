Play & Learn

Fun activities that help little adventurers grow smarter, kinder, and more curious!

Choose an Adventure!

Explore games and creative challenges with Alphy. Every activity is made to inspire imagination and joyful learning!

  • Coloring Pages

    Grab your crayons! Bring Alphy’s adventures to life with fun coloring sheets.

  • Story Sounds

    Listen to giggles, roars, and whispers from Alphy’s world — perfect for storytime!

  • Alphy’s Quiz

    Can you beat Alphy’s quiz? Test your smarts with silly and fun questions!

  • Puzzle Time

    Match shapes, find hidden paths, and solve mini riddles with Alphy!

  • Word Builder

    Build simple words and learn letters through laughter and games.

  • Memory Match

    Flip the cards and find Alphy’s friends — can you remember where they are?
