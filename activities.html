Play & Learn

Fun activities that help little adventurers grow smarter, kinder, and more curious!

Choose an Adventure!

Play, learn, and explore with Alphy! Each activity brings imagination, discovery, and joy to every young explorer.

  • Coloring Pages

    Grab your crayons and bring Alphy’s adventures to life with fun, printable coloring sheets!

  • Story Sounds

    Listen to giggles, roars, and magical story sounds straight from Alphy’s jungle world!

  • Alphy’s Quiz

    Test your knowledge with fun questions and learn something new with every quiz!

  • Puzzle Time

    Fit pieces together, find hidden shapes, and solve fun puzzles with Alphy!

  • Word Building

    Help Alphy spell and learn new words through exciting word-building games!

  • Memory Match

    Flip the cards and find matching pairs — how fast can you match all of Alphy’s friends?
